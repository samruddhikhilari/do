VIT Course Projects

Software Development Project (SDP)

The Software Development Project (SDP) is a comprehensive course that focuses on the complete software development life cycle. Students work in teams to develop real-world software applications, following industry best practices and methodologies.

Key Features:
  • Team-based software development
  • Requirement analysis and documentation
  • Agile development methodologies
  • Version control and collaboration
  • Testing and deployment

Engineering Design and Innovation (EDI)

Engineering Design and Innovation focuses on developing creative solutions to real-world problems through systematic design thinking and innovation methodologies.

Key Aspects:
  • Design thinking process
  • Prototyping and testing
  • User-centered design
  • Innovation management
  • Project presentation and pitching

Design Thinking (DT)

Design Thinking is a human-centered approach to innovation that draws from the designer's toolkit to integrate the needs of people, the possibilities of technology, and the requirements for business success.

Core Principles:
  • Empathy with users
  • Problem definition
  • Ideation and brainstorming
  • Prototyping
  • Testing and iteration
